Medicine Recommendations

Symptom: Body Pain

Ibuprofen

NSAID for pain, fever, and inflammation.

Adult Dose: 200–400 mg every 4–6 hrs; max 1200 mg/day OTC.

Child Dose: 5–10 mg/kg every 6–8 hrs.

Paracetamol

Pain reliever and fever reducer.

Adult Dose: 500–1000 mg every 4–6 hrs; max 4g/day.

Child Dose: 15 mg/kg every 4–6 hrs.

Topical Analgesic Gel

For localized muscle and joint pain.

Adult Dose: Apply as directed on packaging.

Child Dose: Apply as directed (check pediatric guidance).
